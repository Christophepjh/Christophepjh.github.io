Week 8 - Festival of Economics

In week 8 I created two charts to support or refute an idea in the Festival of Economics.

This was inspired by George Matson who mentioned the decoupling effect between a countries wealth and CO2 emissions. You can see this effect with the Loess regression.

Chart one shows the relationship between GDP per capita and CO2 emissions across the world.

Chart two shows the relationship of GDP growth and CO2 emissions growth in France from the 1820s to the 2010s.

Both graphs support the idea that C02 emissions and Wealth are decoupling.
